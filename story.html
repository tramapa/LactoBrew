The LactoBrew Journey

LactoBrew began as a dream in the heart of Surabaya, where a group of coffee enthusiasts sought to redefine the coffee experience. Inspired by Indonesia’s rich coffee culture, we set out to craft a beverage that balances bold flavors with creamy indulgence, all while honoring local farmers and sustainable practices. Our 18-hour cold-brew process was perfected through countless experiments, resulting in the smooth, flavorful drink you love today.

From our first bottle sold at a local market to becoming a beloved part of Surabaya’s daily rhythm, LactoBrew is a story of passion, perseverance, and community. Join us as we continue to write this story, one sip at a time.
